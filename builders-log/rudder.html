Rudder Construction

← Back to Build Log

The rudder is the first component of the empennage (tail section) assembly. It's where the learning curve hits hardest—back-riveting stiffeners, fabricating spacers, drilling skins, dealing with part variations across 20-year-old kits, and discovering why battery drills have no place in aircraft construction.

This section documents every session, every mistake, every recovery. The parts that don't fit. The tools that fail. The moments where Ian Warburton's phone call saved the day. This is the unfiltered rudder build.
